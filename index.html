How We Get The Data

The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) now, since 1 January 2019, requires hospitals to post their prices for standard charges and update them at least once a year. To further improve price transparency for patients, the hospitals must post them online for easier access.

For further reading: https://s3.amazonaws.com/public-inspection.federalregister.gov/2018-08705.pdf
